Fermented beans(natto)

What are the ingredients?

Shared ingredients:
Soybeans
Rice
Green onion

Unique ingriedients:
Bacillus backeria(backteria that makes soybeans sticky)
Water

Recipe:

  1. Put soybeans in a bowl with cold water(500g soybeans: 2L water) for 24 hours.
  2. After 24 hours, simmer them for 3 to 4 hours using a pot.
  3. Put them into a bowl, and mix with warm water and Bacillus backeria.
  4. Put them into a container, and leave in a refrigerator. Wait for 24 hours until it gets fermentated.
  5. Now we can eat fermentated soybeans with green onions as a topping, and put over rice!
